# Function design document HTML CSS


## Functional requirements
* Buy button to add products to your cart
* Cart where you can see what products you bought, how much their total price is per category, and what the total price of all the products together products is.
* Out of stock function where if the number of products goes to 0 and shows "Out of stock!". 
* If you click an out of stock item button, it won't have any effects (meaning it won't add to the cart and won't go below 0)

## Non-functional requirements
* Use the data in the JavaScript file to give the products their price and stock
* Make 4 different boxes, three for items that are sold, one for the cart.
